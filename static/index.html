How TDEElytics Works

1. The Math: 1kg of fat equals roughly 7,700 calories. Standard calculators guess your burn rate based on height/weight.

2. The Algorithm: This app ignores guesses. It tracks exactly how many calories you eat, and compares it to exactly how much your "Trend Weight" changes over a 14-day rolling window.

3. The Result: By day 14, the app calculates your true, dynamic metabolic burn rate (TDEE) based on thermodynamics, and auto-adjusts your daily calorie targets perfectly so you never hit a plateau.
